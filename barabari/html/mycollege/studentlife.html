STUDENTLIFE

Student life at Vivekananda Government Degree College (VGDC) is a mix of academic rigour and a strong emphasis on social responsibility and skill-building. The campus culture is defined by its diverse student body and a "secular environment" that encourages creative thinking

Extracurriculars & Clubs

Support Services: Students participate in the NSS (National Service Scheme) and Women Empowerment Cell (WEC), which host awareness workshops and social service events. Cultural Activities: The college celebrates festivals like Bathukamma and national days such as Rashtriya Ektha Diwas. Student Groups: Active departmental clubs (e.g., Political Science) organize group discussions, quizzes, and awareness programmes like International Democracy Day and National Voters Day

Sports & Fitness

Competitive Sports: The college is known for its strong Volleyball and Kabaddi teams, which regularly compete at the university and district levels (e.g., Yuvatarangam competitions). On-Campus Facilities: There is a gymnasium and a sports complex for indoor and outdoor games like chess and carroms

Campus Amenities

Academic Hubs: A well-equipped library with over 10,000 books and an e-corner serves as a quiet study space. Social Spaces: A campus canteen is available for students and staff to gather during breaks. Digital Access: The campus is Wi-Fi enabled, and many classrooms feature digital boards and projectors for interactive learning

Career Readiness

The Telangana Skill Knowledge Centre (TSKC) is a core part of student life, offering placement training and hosting Job Melas with various companies.
