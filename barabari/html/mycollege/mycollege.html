ABOUT COLLEGE

Vivekananda Government Degree College (VGDC) has a rich history of empowering students from diverse socioeconomic backgrounds since its founding in 1966. Originally established as an evening college by eminent scholar Acharya Khandavalli Lakshmi Ranjanam, it was taken over by the government in 1982 and eventually converted into a day college in 1990.

History of our college

1966 (Founding): Established as an evening college under the name Swami Vivekananda Education Society (SVES) Telugu Kalasala. It was founded by Acharya Khandavalli Lakshmi Ranjanam, a renowned scholar, and chaired by Dr. Marri Chenna Reddy (former Chief Minister of AP). 1982 (Provincialization): The Government of Andhra Pradesh took over the institution, renaming it SVES Government Telugu Kalasala (Evening).
